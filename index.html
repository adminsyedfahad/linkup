Our Story
Your favorite food,
delivered without the hassle.

Connecting You to What Matters, Instantly.

In 2026, a simple idea sparked the beginning of LinkUp.

We realized that if people around the world could build companies that solve everyday problems, there was no reason we couldn't do the same. What started as a vision soon became a mission: to create a platform that makes everyday ordering simple, fast, and reliable.

LinkUp was founded by Syed Fahad, with Syed Abdullah joining as Co-Founder, and Syed Abdulrahman leading the company as Chief Executive Officer (CEO). Together, as brothers sharing the same ambition, we set out to build a platform designed around one goal — putting customers first.

Our mission is to provide seamless, one-click access to food, groceries, cafés, and everyday essentials through a fast, reliable, and user-friendly experience.

But LinkUp is more than just a delivery platform.

We believe local businesses are the heart of every community. That's why we focus on empowering local restaurants, cafés, grocery stores, and independent businesses by helping them reach more customers and grow in the digital world.

Every decision we make is guided by our commitment to convenience, reliability, and customer satisfaction. We continuously strive to create an experience that saves time, simplifies daily life, and connects people with the products and services they love.

As LinkUp grows, our vision remains unchanged: to become the most trusted hyper-local delivery platform, supporting local businesses while delivering exceptional experiences to customers every day.

Built by brothers. Powered by innovation. Driven by community.

❓ Help & Support
Frequently Asked Questions
How do I track my order?▾
Once your order is confirmed, go to Orders in the bottom nav and tap Track Order. You'll see your rider's live position on the map and step-by-step status updates.
Can I change my delivery address after ordering?▾
You can update your address before the restaurant confirms your order. Once confirmed, please contact the rider directly via the Chat option on the tracking page.
What payment methods are accepted?▾
LinkUp accepts Cash on Delivery (COD), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Debit/Credit Cards, and LinkUp Points (earned on every order). Online payments are processed securely via Razorpay.
My order is late — what should I do?▾
Check the live tracking page for your rider's current location. If the delay is unusual, use the Call or Chat button on the tracking screen to reach your rider directly, or contact us via the support options below.
How do I cancel an order?▾
Orders can be cancelled within 2 minutes of placing them. Go to Orders → View Order and tap Cancel Order. After 2 minutes, please contact support as the restaurant may have already started preparing.
How do I get a refund?▾
Refunds are processed to your LinkUp Wallet (instant) or original payment method (3–5 business days). Wallet refunds appear instantly in your ⭐ My Wallet section. Contact support with your Order ID.
