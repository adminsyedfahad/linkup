01

Overview

LinkUp ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our food delivery platform at orderlinkup.vercel.app.

We operate in Hyderabad, Telangana, India, and comply with applicable Indian data protection laws including the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA).

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

03

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide and improve our service:

  • Order processing: Confirming, preparing, and delivering your order
  • Real-time tracking: Sharing your order status and rider location with you via your unique tracking link
  • Customer support: Resolving issues, processing refunds, and responding to queries
  • Notifications: Sending order status updates via the app
  • Analytics: Understanding usage patterns to improve our platform
  • Legal compliance: Meeting obligations under applicable law

We do not use your data for automated profiling or make legally significant decisions about you based solely on automated processing.

04

Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Delivery Riders: Your name, phone number, and delivery address are shared with the assigned rider to complete your delivery
  • Restaurant Partners: Your order items and special instructions are shared with the restaurant preparing your food
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and hosting services. Google's privacy policy applies to data processed through their infrastructure
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government authority

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any unrelated third parties.

05

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers, which are protected by enterprise-grade security including encryption at rest and in transit (TLS/SSL).

We implement the following security measures:

  • Firestore Security Rules to prevent unauthorized data access
  • Firebase Authentication for secure login
  • Path-based access tokens (unguessable 24-character strings) for order tracking
  • Admin access restricted to verified email only

We retain your order data for a period of 2 years for legal and accounting purposes, after which it may be anonymized or deleted. You may request earlier deletion — see Your Rights below.

No method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

06

Cookies & Local Storage

LinkUp uses browser localStorage and sessionStorage (not traditional cookies) to improve your experience:

  • Order state: Storing your active order ID and tracking token so you can return to your order status
  • Theme preference: Remembering your light/dark mode choice
  • Phone number: Pre-filling your number on return visits (stored locally on your device only)
  • Wallet token: Your unique wallet identifier for cashback and rewards
  • Visit tracking: A session flag to count unique visits (not your identity)

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

We may also use Firebase Analytics to collect aggregated, anonymized usage data to understand how our service is used.

07

Your Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA) and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with us or the Data Protection Board of India
  • Nomination: Nominate another person to exercise your rights in case of your incapacity

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@orderlinkup.in. We will respond within 30 days.

08

Children's Privacy

LinkUp is not intended for use by children under the age of 18 years. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete the information.
